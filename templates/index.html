How To Play

Welcome to CS50 Wordy! The rules are simple: pick a difficulty you want to play and try guessing the random word! After each guess color indicators will appear providing you information to guess the random word. You have 6 tries to guess the word. Good luck!

Green: This letter is in the correct place of the random word.

Orange: This letter exists, but is currently in the wrong place of the random word.

No changes in color: The letter does not exist in the random word.
